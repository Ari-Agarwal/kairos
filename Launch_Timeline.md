# Telos Launch Timeline — Go-to-Market (Jun 27–Aug 11)

Runs in parallel with [Software_Timeline.md](Software_Timeline.md), same 7 weeks, same Aug 11 end date.

**Scope change (Jun 28):** the MVP is student-only — no counselor dashboard, no school sales, no premium/billing at launch. This rewrites the GTM strategy from "build a warm school pipeline for a fall signing conversation" to "get students and parents using a free product directly." School/counselor sales motion (associations, district pitches, DPA, press angle aimed at counseling offices) moves to the **Phase 2** section at the bottom, to be picked back up once the counselor dashboard and premium tier exist.

The reasoning for the new approach: a free, direct-to-student product doesn't need an institutional buying cycle or a summer-dark counseling office to be ready before it's useful — it can get real usage the moment it launches. Optimize for getting students to actually try it and tell other students, not for landing a school contract.

Parked, not deleted. Pick this back up once the counselor dashboard and premium tier (Software_Timeline Phase 2) actually exist — pitching schools on a product they can't yet use wastes the relationship.
