## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

Public high schools with 800–2,500 students and a counselor-to-student ratio worse than 1:300
(national average is ~1:385, so this targets schools where counselors are visibly stretched),
located in suburban or mid-size-district areas where families expect college guidance but the
school can't staff enough 1:1 counseling time to deliver it. Secondary fit: private/independent
schools with strong college-prep expectations from parents but a small counseling staff relative
to class size. Primary buyer is the lead counselor or counseling department head; budget likely
needs district or site-admin sign-off for anything beyond a single-counselor pilot.
